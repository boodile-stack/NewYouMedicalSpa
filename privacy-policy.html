Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

At NewYou Medical Spa, your privacy is treated with the same care and discretion as our services. We are committed to protecting your personal information and maintaining the highest standards of confidentiality, transparency, and security.

This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, book services, or interact with us online or in person.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information including, but not limited to:

  • Full name, email address, phone number, and mailing address
  • Appointment details and service preferences
  • Payment information (processed securely through third-party providers)
  • Medical or treatment-related information necessary to safely provide services
  • Website usage data such as IP address, browser type, and interaction patterns

All information collected is used solely to enhance your experience and ensure safe, personalized care.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Schedule and manage appointments
  • Customize treatment plans and services
  • Communicate important updates, confirmations, or promotions (with consent)
  • Process payments securely
  • Improve our website, services, and client experience
  • Comply with medical, legal, and regulatory requirements

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

Medical & Confidential Information

Any medical or treatment-related information you provide is handled with strict confidentiality and used only for clinical, safety, and documentation purposes. Access to this information is limited to authorized professionals directly involved in your care.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard safeguards to protect your information, including secure systems, encrypted payment processing, and restricted access protocols. While no system can guarantee absolute security, we take every reasonable measure to protect your data with care and diligence.

Third-Party Services

Our website may use trusted third-party services for scheduling, payments, analytics, or communication. These providers are granted access only to the information necessary to perform their services and are required to maintain confidentiality and security standards consistent with ours.

Cookies & Website Analytics

Our website may use cookies or similar technologies to enhance functionality and improve user experience. These tools help us understand how visitors interact with our site and allow us to refine our digital experience. You may adjust your browser settings to manage or disable cookies at any time.

Your Rights & Choices

You have the right to:

  • Request access to or correction of your personal information
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time
  • Request clarification on how your data is used

Certain medical or transactional records may be retained as required by law or professional regulations.

Updates to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Updates will be posted on this page, and continued use of our website constitutes acceptance of those changes.
